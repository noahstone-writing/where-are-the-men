4:1 and Counting

Why Single Women Vastly Outnumber Men on the Mission Field

Noah Stone

Single women have outnumbered single men on the evangelical mission field for more than a century, and the gap is widest exactly where the gospel is least available. This forum talk and the research behind it ask why — and what the local church can do.
